Our History

Founded in 2023 when founder Kieran Bradford didn't know what to make the business for the website he was making Kieran's fascination with cars sparked an undying enthusiasm that transcended into adulthood. Determined to share this passion with others, he embarked on a mission to create a haven where fellow enthusiasts could indulge in their automotive fantasies. What started as a personal collection quickly evolved into a thriving business, driven by Kieran's dedication to sourcing and offering the most remarkable toy cars from around the globe. Today, Kieran's Toy Car Sales stands as a testament to one man's dream turned into a vibrant reality.
